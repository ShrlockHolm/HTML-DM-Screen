Home Combat Exploring Special Circumstances

Do the following

1 ACTION
A list of possible actions are given in the section titled “Actions.”
1 REACTION
Usually spent either during a “ready action” or during an attack of opportunity.
1 BONUS ACTION
No generic bonus actions exist. Many race/class abilities or magic items allow for specific bonus actions.
1 MOVE
Move at your current speed. Take into account effects listed in the “Movement” section.
FREE ACTIONS
Drawing a sword, pulling out a potion, etc. don't require an action. Use any reasonable number of free actions on your turn.
