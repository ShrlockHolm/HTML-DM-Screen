Melee Attacks

TO HIT d20 + STR + Weapon proficiency
DAMAGE Weapon dice + STR
REACH
Unless otherwise noted, melee attacks can reach a target up to 5 feet away from the attacker.
FINESSE WEAPONS
Use DEX instead of STR to hit.
VERSATILE WEAPONS
Can be weilded either one-handed or two-handed. Use the lesser of teh damage dice for one-handed, and the greater for two-handed
THROWN WEAPONS
Some melee weapons can be thrown as well. When you do, use the rules of a ranged attack.

Ranged Attacks

TO HIT d20 + DEX + Weapon proficiency
DAMAGE Weapon dice + DEX
RANGE - A(B)
A - Normal range. If a target is within this range, roll normally
B - Long range. if a target is outside the normal range but within long range, roll the attack with disadvantage.
SPELLS
Many spells require making a ranged attack, but do not follow the typical guidelines of physical attacks. See the section on magic for more info. TODO HYPERLINK

Surprise

When attempting to surprise another creature perform a stealth vs. perception contest. If you win, you have surprised the other creature.

Surprised creatures cannot act during the first round of combat.

Criticals

FUMBLE

CRITICAL HIT
Natural 20 → Automatically hits

Special Movement Rules

OVERRUN or TUMBLE: Contest STR or DEX to move through an opponent's space. PHB xxx
BREAKING UP MOVEMENT: During a round a character can move a distance equal to their speed, but that movement does not have to happen all at once. PHB xxx
MOVING PAST FRIENDS AND FOES: You can move through friends freely. You can move through hostile creature's space only if the creature is at least two sizes larger or smaller than you. In such a case the creature's space treated as difficult terrain. PHB xxx
DIFFICULT TERRAIN: Traversing difficult terrain increases movement cost by 1 foot per each foot moved. If no other movement penalties apply, this halves movement speed. If other conditions apply, the effects are cumulative. PHB xxx
PRONE: Dropping prone is a free action. Standing from prone costs half movement speed. PHB xxx
MOVEMENT WHILE PRONE: Crawling costs an extra feet of speed per foot moved (1 foot movement costs 2 feet). Crawling through difficult terrain costs an additional foot of speed (1 foot movement costs 3 feet). PHB xxx
SQUEEZING: A creature or player can move through spaces one size class lower, but may only move up to half their speed and suffer disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saves. Attacks against a squeezed entity have advantage. PHB xxx

Grappling

ACTION
Contest athletics against the opponent's acrobatics or athletics (opponent's choice). If you succeed your target suffers the "grappled" condition.
*The target must be no more than 1 size category larger than you and must be within reach.
CONDITION
Speed = 0 regardless of bonuses. Condition ends when grappler is incapacitated or when an effect removes the grappler from reach.
MOVING
You can drag or carry the grappled creature, but your speed is halved unless the creature is two or more sizes smaller than you.
ESCAPE
Make an athletics or acrobatics check contested against the grappler's athletics check to escape grapple.
