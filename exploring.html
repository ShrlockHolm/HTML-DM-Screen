Follow these steps to make a custom background for a chacter. (DMG 289)

  1. Root it in your world
    Make it topical
  2. Suggest Personal Characteristics
    Write something to go in the personality traits boxes
  3. Assign Proficiencies or Languages
    2 skill proficiencies and 2 languages or tool proficiencies
  4. Include Starting Equipment
    Include some random crap that you get at the beginning and starting money to buy better crap
  5. Settle on a Background Feature
    Make a feature that will get you non-mechanical bonuses when role-playing. E.G. people at the library like you so you get in for free. (but libraries are free anyway?)
