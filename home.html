The Core Mechanic

Key Rules

DIS/ADVANTAGE
For advantage, roll 2d20, take the better roll. For Disadvantage, take the worse.
GROUP CHECKS
Everyone attempting the same action rolls. If at least half the group beats the DC, the effort succeeds.
PASSIVE CHECKS
10 + Modifiers + Advantage (+5) or Disadvantage (-5).
CONTESTS
Both parties roll as normal, but success is compared to the opposing party's roll rather than a DC. If there is a tie, the situation remains unchanged, or neither party succeeds.
INSPIRATION
Grants a player advantage to any one roll. The DM may award a player an inspiration bonus when they role-play their character exceptionally well. You can never have more than one Inspiration. Players can give their Inspiration to others.
FRACTIONS

Exotic Damage

EXHAUSTION
Finishing a long rest reduces exhaustion level by 1 assuming the creature has had food and drink.
FALLING
Take 1d6 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet fallen, max 20d6. Land prone unless damage is avoided.
SUFFOCATING
Creature can hold breath equal to minutes = 1 + CON modifier (minimum 30 seconds). At start of next turn after this time has elapsed, drop to 0 HP and dying.

Levels of Exhaustion

1 Disadvantage on ability checks.
2 Speed halved
3 Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws
4 HP maximum halved
5 Speed reduced to 0
