Home Combat Exploring Special Circumstances
RANGED ATTACKS Ranged weapons have two range numbers: 1st number is normal range, 2nd is long range. You roll with disadvantage at long range. PHB xxx
OPPORTUNITY ATTACK Moving beyond a creature's reach provokes opportunity attack. The first creature can use a reaction to make a melee attack against the one moving away. PHB xxx
RANGED ATTACKS IN MELEE When hostile creature is within 5 feet you get disadvantage on attack roll. PHB xxx
TWO WEAPON FIGHTING If attacking with a light melee weapon that one hand, use a bonus action to attack with a different light melee weapon that in the other hand. Ability modifiers are not added to the damage of the bonus attack, unless that modifier is negative. If either weapon has the thrown property, it may be thrown instead of making a melee attack. PHB xxx
GROUP DAMAGE ROLLS If spell of effect deals damage to more than one target at same time, roll damage once for all targets. PHB xxx
IMPROVISED WEAPONS Do 1d4 damage. PHB xxx
NONLETHAL DAMAGE PC declares they're dealing nonlethal damage. Deal damage as normal, if reduce target to 0 HP or fewer, target simply has 0 HP and falls unconscious. PHB xxx
CRITICAL HITS/FAILURES Natural 1 → Auto Fail. Natural 20 → Roll all of the attack's damage dice twice and add them together. Then add any relevant modifiers as normal. Spells withattack rolls can also have a critical hit or failure. PHB xxx
RESISTANCE/VULNERABILITY Damage from the specified type of damage is halved/doubled. PHB xxx
OVERRUN or TUMBLE: Contest STR or DEX to move through an opponent's space. PHB xxx
BREAKING UP MOVEMENT: During a round a character can move a distance equal to their speed, but that movement does not have to happen all at once. PHB xxx
MOVING PAST FRIENDS AND FOES: You can move through friends freely. You can move through hostile creature's space only if the creature is at least two sizes larger or smaller than you. In such a case the creature's space treated as difficult terrain. PHB xxx
DIFFICULT TERRAIN: Traversing difficult terrain increases movement cost by 1 foot per each foot moved. If no other movement penalties apply, this halves movement speed. If other conditions apply, the effects are cumulative. PHB xxx
PRONE: Dropping prone is a free action. Standing from prone costs half movement speed. PHB xxx
MOVEMENT WHILE PRONE: Crawling costs an extra feet of speed per foot moved (1 foot movement costs 2 feet). Crawling through difficult terrain costs an additional foot of speed (1 foot movement costs 3 feet). PHB xxx
SQUEEZING: A creature or player can move through spaces one size class lower, but may only move up to half their speed and suffer disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saves. Attacks against a squeezed entity have advantage. PHB xxx

Dying & Healing

DEATH SAVING THROWS Roll 1d20 with no modifiers, DC10. After three successes, the creature becomes stable. After three failures, the creature dies. Successes and failures are cumulative until stable or dead. PHB XXX
IMMEDIATE DEATH If a character at 0 HP is dealt damage equal to their total HP, that character immediately dies, regardless of previous death saving throws. PHB XXX
FIRST AID DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check to use first aid to stabilize without healer's kit. PHB XXX
MEDICINE SKILL A dying creature or player can be stabilized with a DC 10 medicine check. PHB 76
STABILIZED If stabilized, creature is unconscious but no longer makes death saving throws. If not healed, will remain unconscious until regain 1 HP after 1d4 hours have passed (at which point you regain 1 HP). PHB XXX
STABILIZING A CREATURE You can use your action to administer first aid to an unconscious creature and attempt to stabilize it, which requires a successful DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check. PHB XXX
HEALERS KIT Has 10 uses. You can expend one use of the kit, as an action, to stabilize a creature that has 0 hit points without doing a Wisdom (Medicine) check. PHB XXX
HEALING While HP < 0, healing frst brings HP to 0, then adds HP as normal (e.g. you're at -5 HP, cure light wounds grants 8 HP, you now have 8 HP). PHB XXX

Resting

SPENDING HD WHILE STABILIZED Not in the rules but clarified by the designers: a stabilized PC (i.e. at 0 hp) can spend HD (if he has any remaining) after a 1 hour short rest even though the PC will regain 1 HP after 1d4 hours. PHB XXX
